Redirection

How to Set This Up in Chrome

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://settings/searchEngines.
  2. Under Site search, click Add.
  3. Configure the fields as follows:
    • Search engine: Custom Redirect
    • Shortcut: r (or your preferred shortcut keyword)
    • URL with %s in place of query:
      https://yourdomain.com/redirect.html?dict=https://yourdomain.com/dict.js&to=%s
  4. Click Save. Now typing r gmail in your address bar will instantly route you.

How to Modify the Address List

To change, add, or remove shortcuts, edit your external dict.js file. Update the pairs inside the keywordUrls object following this structure:

"shortcut": "https://destination-url.com",
